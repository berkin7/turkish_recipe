Menemen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 medium tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 green pepper (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: Red pepper flakes, feta cheese

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped green pepper and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Crack the eggs into the pan and stir gently to scramble.
  5. Season with salt, black pepper, and optional red pepper flakes.
  6. Cook until the eggs are just set, then remove from heat.
  7. Serve warm with crusty bread. Optionally, garnish with feta cheese.

Lahmacun Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 pound ground lamb or beef
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 medium tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons parsley (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and water to form a soft dough. Knead until smooth, then let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  3. In another bowl, combine ground meat, onion, tomato, tomato paste, parsley, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll out each ball into a thin round.
  5. Spread the meat mixture thinly over each dough round.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are crisp and the meat is cooked.
  7. Serve hot with a squeeze of lemon and fresh parsley.
